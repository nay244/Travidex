Circular completion ring — for Country cards, Stats detail, and chunk tiles.

```jsx
<CompletionRing found={6} total={9} size={64} />
<CompletionRing found={140} total={140} size={48}>🇯🇵</CompletionRing>
```

Green at 100% (claimed, glows), amber partial, dim at 0. Center defaults to the percentage; pass `children` for a flag or count.
